## Rules

- `fresh`: up to 24 hours old.
- `recent`: over 24 hours and up to 7 days old.
- `stale`: over 7 days old.
- `unknown`: missing or invalid evidence time.
- Stale/unknown evidence must be labelled historical or undated and requiring fresh verification.
- Even fresh evidence must be verified before price or commitment use.
