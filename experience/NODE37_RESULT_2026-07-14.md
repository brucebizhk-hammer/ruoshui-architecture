# Node 37 - Dual WeChat Subject And Memory Isolation

Status: deployed on 2026-07-14.

## Owner Principle

- `ruoshui` and `songsheng` are two people and two independent front-office identities.
- They belong to one company, so reviewed business analysis may be shared explicitly.
- Private conversation memory, relationship state, tone, promises and reply context must remain independent.

## Data Model

- `contacts`: canonical person for optional company-backend identity linking.
- `account_relationship_profiles`: relationship per WeChat account and subject.
- `conversations.identity_id`: front-office subject (`ruoshui` or `songsheng`).
- `memory_items.identity_id`: subject-private memory.
- `company_shared/company`: explicit shared analysis scope only.
