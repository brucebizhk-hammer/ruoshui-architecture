# Node 22 - Reply Delivery Reliability

Status: completed and deployed on 2026-07-13.

## Problem confirmed

- Failed Core deliveries were retried every 10 seconds without backoff.
- Short Core/Hermes outages therefore produced retry storms and delayed newer messages.
- Historical delivery logs showed delays of roughly 3 minutes for a direct message and 21 minutes for a group message.

## Implementation

- Added `message_queue.next_retry_at` as a backward-compatible SQLite migration.
- Pending and claim operations now require the retry deadline to be due.
- Failures use exponential backoff: 10, 30, 60, 120, 300, then 600 seconds capped.
- Messages remain queued; no silent drop or dead-letter deletion was introduced.
- Successful delivery clears retry state.
