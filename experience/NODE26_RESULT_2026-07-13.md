Status: complete and deployed on 2026-07-13.

## Mainline Gap Closed

Node 25 could stage refresh requests but had no bounded execution lifecycle. A scheduled Worker can now claim one request, apply a public-query safety gate, fetch through the existing Exa Provider, store raw evidence and attach evidence IDs to the request.

## Existing Components Extended

- Reuses `public_evidence_refresh_requests`, Exa Provider, `LeadRepository`, `raw_web_evidence` and the existing freshness query.
- No new evidence database, reply engine or customer store.

## Safety And Lifecycle

- One request per scheduled run; task interval 10 minutes; execution limit 5 minutes.
- Phone numbers, wxids, chatroom IDs, long numeric identifiers and sensitive private topics never reach public search.
- Sensitive requests become `needs_review` with an audit reason.
- Provider failure returns to pending until attempt 3, then becomes failed.
- Success stores evidence IDs and a compact result audit.
- Worker writes raw public evidence only: no Lead promotion, customer/profile write, group fact confirmation or WeChat action.
