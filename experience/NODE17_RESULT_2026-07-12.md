## Delivered

- Low-urgency group-profile digests wait for an explicit time window before delivery.
- Default digest window: 300 seconds, configurable with `GROUP_PROFILE_DIGEST_WINDOW_SECONDS`.
- Default maximum digest size: 10 items, configurable with `GROUP_PROFILE_DIGEST_MAX_ITEMS` and capped at 20.
- Price and candidate-task notifications remain immediate and bypass the digest window.
- Retry rows bypass the waiting window so a failed delivery is not delayed again.
- Digest rows beyond the maximum are split into bounded deliveries without losing per-row audit state.
- `/health` exposes the active digest window and maximum item count.
